Our Story

HOW IT ALL BEGAN

It was a quiet Tuesday afternoon at a local coffee shop where our story began. Jane was deeply engrossed in a book, and James, captivated by her focus, found the courage to ask what she was reading. That simple question sparked a conversation that lasted for hours.

From shared laughter over spilled coffee to discovering a mutual love for hiking and old movies, we found a universe in each other. Our journey has been filled with adventure, growth, and an ever-deepening love. We can't wait to start our next chapter as husband and wife.
